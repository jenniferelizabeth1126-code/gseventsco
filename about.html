About

Jennifer Jones

Event Strategy Consultant · Venue Consulting Expert · Strategic Advisor

The Consultant Behind GS Events

Built on 26 Years of Getting It Done at the Highest Level

Jennifer Jones doesn't just plan events — she builds the strategy behind them. With over two and a half decades in the hospitality and events industry, she has earned a reputation as the professional that other professionals call when they need it done right.

Her career spans every dimension of the events world: from managing multimillion-dollar incentive programs for Fortune 500 companies as a Senior Account Executive at CSI DMC, to directing the events department at prestigious Los Angeles properties like Farmhouse LA and The Mayfair Hotel. From founding Visionary Consulting Group to serving as Director of Education for MPI Southern California — Jennifer has consistently operated at the intersection of strategy, leadership, and hospitality excellence.

In 2025, Jennifer launched Gold Standard Events — a rebranded, elevated consulting firm designed to do one thing exceptionally well: be the trusted strategic partner that corporate meeting planners and hospitality venues rely on when results are non-negotiable.

She is known for her calm leadership under pressure, her instinctive ability to read a room — and a market — and her uncompromising commitment to delivering the Gold Standard experience every time.

Philosophy

"The event is the vehicle. The strategy is what gets you there."

Every engagement Jennifer takes on begins with a single question: what does success actually look like for you? The logistics follow. The strategy leads.
